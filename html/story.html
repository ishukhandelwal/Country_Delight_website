Our Story

50 years back, life was very simple. We used to have a cow in our backyard that gave us natural and fresh milk everyday. There was never a need to worry about adulteration since we owned the animal. Today, the milk we consume is either reconstituted/recombined with milk powder or takes days to reach our home post milking which is prone to adulteration.

Started in 2015, Milk Dairy is a bunch of Engineers and IIM Graduates who aim to bring back the basics of Milk. Milk Dairy promises natural, fresh and unadulterated milk directly to the doorstep of the consumer. Consumer ordering and servicing happens through a first-of-its-kind mobile application that completely takes away the hassle of managing ones milk requirements.
